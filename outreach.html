Community Outreach

Trinity Hearing is dedicated to reaching remote and underserved communities to provide hearing services through mobile clinics and outreach programs.

Mobile Screening

We organize mobile clinics to reach rural areas and provide free hearing screenings for all community members.

Sensitization Campaigns

Our team conducts campaigns to raise awareness about hearing health and prevention methods in local communities.

Home Visits

For individuals unable to visit our clinics, we offer home visits for hearing assessments and follow-up care.
