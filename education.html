Education & Advocacy

We believe that awareness is key. Our education programs aim to raise awareness of hearing loss prevention and advocate for the inclusion of people with hearing impairments in society.

Preventable Hearing Loss

We focus on educating communities about preventable hearing loss and the importance of protecting hearing health.

Safe Listening Habits

Our programs encourage safe listening practices to prevent noise-induced hearing loss, especially in young people.

Advocacy for Inclusion

We work to ensure that people with hearing impairments are fully included in all aspects of life and have access to necessary support and services.
