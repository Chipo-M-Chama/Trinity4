Our Story

Founded in 2015, Trinity Hearing Institute began as a small hearing care center in Lusaka, driven by the passion to address hearing impairment in underserved communities. Over the years, it has grown into a multi-branch, nationally recognized institution offering a full spectrum of audiological services. With cutting-edge technology, outreach initiatives, and strong partnerships, we’ve become a lifeline for thousands of clients across Zambia.

Key Achievements

  • Over 20,000 patients served since inception
  • Launched Zambia's first mobile ear-care van
  • Introduced low-cost digital hearing aids
  • Developed training programs for rural healthcare providers

Recognitions & Awards

  • 2022 Ministry of Health Award for Rural Innovation
  • Starkey Excellence in Outreach Award (2021)
  • Zambia Healthcare Impact Recognition (2020)
